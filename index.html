Spread sheet to JSON converter

Enter the ID of your google spread sheet in the box above and click the button. Remember to share your spread sheet ("Anyone with the link can view"), and see to that it’s published: That’s in the file-menu > "Publish to the web".
If you click the the word "sheet" in the big text, a new tab opens up with a test spread sheet. Copy the ID from that and try to generate some JSON!
